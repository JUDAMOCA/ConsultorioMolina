# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Colombian dental patients and the clinic admin. Patients book appointments from their phone or laptop; the admin (dentist/receptionist) monitors new bookings and confirmed payments from a desktop panel.

## Product Purpose

Online appointment booking for Consultorio Molina, a Colombian dental clinic. Patients browse services, pick a time slot, and pay online via Wompi or in person. The clinic admin gets a real-time panel with appointment tracking and earnings.

## Brand Personality

Professional, reliable, approachable — "profesional, confiable, cercano". Trustworthy and technically capable while remaining warm and human, not cold or clinical.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (hero metrics, identical card grids, gradient text)
- Cold, clinical hospital aesthetics
- Overly rounded consumer-app designs

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity first** — critical info (date, service, cost) must be instantly scannable
2. **Server-authoritative** — design trusts the backend; never show unconfirmed state
3. **Warm professionalism** — sky-500 primary keeps it trustworthy; copy stays human
4. **Minimal surface** — every element earns its space; no decorative noise

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Spanish-language primary. Colombian date/currency formats.
